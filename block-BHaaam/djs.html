Bounce (Eston)

Eston, commonly known as "Bounce" amongst the Perth DJ elite, has over 15 years experience in the industry and over 18 years in the craft of DJing. He has a strong passion for quality Neo-soul, Golden Era Hiphop, Smooth R&B and Lovers Rock Reggae, as well as a high appreciation for Motown, Funk and Disco classics. His residencies range from maintaining long-term commercial pub gigs at legendary Perth hotels such as The Aberdeen and The Leederville, to holding down huge Urban club nights at some of Perth's biggest clubs including The Library and Metro City, to currently spinning funk soul and rare grooves alongside some of Perth's most popular bars including The Camfield, The Print Hall and The Garden. He has DJ'ed Internationally in countries such as Mexico, Brazil, Argentina and Colombia and has also supported International Artists of the likes of Snoop Dogg, Nelly, Jeremih, Ja Rule, Eve, Ashanti, Mya and Naughty By Nature. Despite his regular appearances at bars, concerts and clubs, Bounce thoroughly enjoys DJing at Weddings and Functions where he can provide a more intimate performance and embrace the celebration. Some of the venues he has serviced around Perth include Crown Casino, Ambrose Estate, Matilda Bay Foreshore, Royal Fresh Water Bay and Nedlands Yacht Clubs, St George Gardens, Quarry Farm, Millbrook Winery, Sitella, Sandalford Wines, Oak Over Grounds, Lamont's Bishop House, Fraser's Kings Park and Caversham House to name a few. No stranger to the Margaret River region, Eston has also serviced private events at Olio Bello, Aravina Estate, Wills Domain, Flutes and Black Brewery. If you are after a quality DJ who can cater to any crowd the search ends at DJ Bounce.

DJ Leche (Lesdter)

Lesdter, otherwise known as "Leche" (pronounced "Leh-cheh) is a hard working force within the DJ circuit. The youngest DJ on the Soul Sound roster, Leche represents that new skool flavour, however do not be fooled by this freshman. As a music connoisseur, Leche has a deep appreciation for all things funky and creates a unique mash-up of styles when he is rocking a dance floor. With over 9 years of experience of club and mobile DJing, combined with his studies at SAE and a very musical upbringing, Leche is best decribed as professional, technical and authentic. Being of South American origin, he is heavily influenced by Latino music and infuses this sound with his passion for quality R&B, Hiphop and Reggae. He has played at popular venues around Perth such as Crown Casino, Metros Concert Club, Metropolis Fremantle, The Monastery, Flawless, Voyeur and the Claremont Hotel, and has also DJ'ed at large scaled events including The ANZ Christmas Party at Ascot, The City to Surf Expo at PCEC and the Grand Opening of Kripsy Kreme in Whitfords. When you book DJ Leche, you don't just book a DJ, you book a fiery fiesta.

DJ Slick (John)

John, more popularly known as "DJ Slick", is one of Perth's hardest working DJs with a strong passion for mashing up urban music with other genres. A DJ in high demand, Slick has a strong fan base here in Perth and understands the pure exhilaration it takes to connect to every listener in the crowd. Slick's love and passion for music is evident through his original productions of genre blending remixes that are guaranteed to fill dance floors. These signature mash-ups are praised and used by DJs worldwide from London to New York, and it is highly likely you have 'gotten down' to a "Slick Mix" yourself. Slick has been a motive force in Perth's Urban Music Scene, being highly involved in "RnB Bounce" Perth's first Urban radio show on Fairfax Media's 96fm and also creating "Shake Underaged Events" which showcased some of Perth's finest nightclub DJs to the youth of Perth. With over 20 years of experience, Slick has played at a long list of venues around Perth, including Varga Lounge, Metro City, The Stables Bar, The Library, Villa and Eve at Crown Casino, just to name a few. On a larger scale, Slick has supported an impressive list of Performing Artists including Rappers 50 cent, Ice Cube, DMX, Snoop Dogg and Nelly; R&B singers such as Kelly Rowland, Akon, Boyz 2 Men, Kci & Jojo and Montell Jordan; and Pop sensations of the likes of Kelly Clarkson, Jessica Maulboy, Havana Brown and the Far East Movement. He has also performed across Australia, playing in Karratha, Brisbane, Melbourne and Sydney and has toured throughout Asia, headlining in Bali, Singapore, Taiwan and Hong Kong.
